Mission

The Homelab Club at Oregon State University is a student-run organization dedicated to helping students build and manage their own home server infrastructure. We provide a collaborative environment where members can learn about system administration, networking, and self-hosted services.

Our club welcomes students of all skill levels and backgrounds. Whether you're just starting your homelab journey or you're an experienced system administrator, our community offers opportunities to learn, share knowledge, and grow your technical skills.

Through hands-on workshops, project collaborations, and regular meetings, we help members develop practical skills in server management, network security, and infrastructure deployment. Our goal is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world implementation.

Values

Hands-on Learning

We believe in learning by doing, providing practical experience with real hardware and infrastructure.

Community Support

We foster a welcoming environment where members can share knowledge, collaborate on projects, and help each other grow.

Technical Excellence

We strive for best practices in system administration, security, and infrastructure management.

Open Source Philosophy

We embrace and contribute to the open-source community, sharing our knowledge and solutions freely.
